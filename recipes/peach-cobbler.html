Mom's Peach Cobbler

This is my mom's peach cobbler. While she has not made this as much recently, this is a childhood favorite of mine. This is best served hot with a side of vanilla ice cream.

    2 teaspoons cornstarch (for thickening)

    1 cup granulated sugar
    1 teaspoon baking powder
    ¼ teaspoon salt
    6 tablespoons unsalted butter, cold and cubed
    ¼ cup boiling water
